# Field Operations Module

## Overview

The Field Operations module tracks all agricultural practices performed on vineyard plots:
cultural practices, phytosanitary applications, fertilization, harvesting, and resource management.

## Cultural Practices

Each **cultural practice** represents a field operation and includes:
- Practice type (pruning, spraying, harvesting, etc.)
- Start date and duration
- Plot and property assignment
- Resources used (workers, equipment, products)
- Hours worked and costs

## Resources

### Human Resources
- Workers and contractors
- Hourly rates
- Licenses and training records

### Equipment
- Tractors, sprayers, harvesters
- Usage hours and maintenance

### Products
- Fertilizers and soil amendments
- Phytopharmaceutical products
- Quantities applied and costs

## Key Views

- **Practice-Resource Full**: Comprehensive view joining practices with all resources
- **HR Calendar**: Monthly calendar of worker hours
- **Equipment Calendar**: Monthly calendar of equipment usage
- **Costs per Property**: Aggregated costs by property

## Onde encontrar na aplicacao

1. Navigate to **Field Operations** in the sidebar
2. Select **Practices** to view all operations
3. Use **Resources** for equipment and worker management
4. Check **Calendar** for monthly scheduling
