# Field Operations — Procedures

## How to Register a Cultural Practice

1. Open **Field Operations > Practices**
2. Click **New Practice**
3. Select **Practice Type** (Pruning, Spraying, Fertilization, Harvesting, etc.)
4. Select the **Plot** and **Date**
5. Add resources:
   - **Workers**: Select workers and enter hours worked
   - **Equipment**: Select equipment and enter usage hours
   - **Products**: Select products (fertilizers, phytopharmaceuticals) and quantities
6. Click **Save**

## How to Register Phytosanitary Applications

1. Open **Field Operations > Phytosanitary**
2. Select the product from the catalog
3. Choose the target plot and date
4. Enter the application rate and treated area
5. Record weather conditions if required
6. Ensure the pre-harvest interval is respected
7. Click **Save**

## How to Register Fertilization

1. Open **Field Operations > Fertilization**
2. Select the fertilizer product
3. Choose the plot and application date
4. Enter the quantity applied (kg/ha)
5. Select application method (broadcast, fertigation, etc.)
6. Click **Save**

## How to Register Harvesting

1. Open **Field Operations > Harvesting**
2. Select the plot and date
3. Enter harvested quantity (kg)
4. Record the destination (cellar intake)
5. Click **Save**

## Onde encontrar na aplicacao

- All operations: **Field Operations > Practices**
- Phytosanitary: **Field Operations > Phytosanitary**
- Fertilization: **Field Operations > Fertilization**
- Harvesting: **Field Operations > Harvesting**
