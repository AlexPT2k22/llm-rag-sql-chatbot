# Cellar Management — Procedures

## How to Register Grape Intake

1. Open **Cellar Management > Intake**
2. Click **New Intake**
3. Select the origin plot and transport
4. Enter the gross weight (kg) and tare
5. Record grape variety
6. Add quality analysis if performed
7. Click **Save**

## How to Create a Wine Lot

1. Open **Cellar Management > Lots**
2. Click **New Lot**
3. Fill in:
   - **Lot Code**: Unique identifier
   - **Wine Type**: Red, White, Rose
   - **Wine Category**: DOC, Vinho Regional, Table Wine
   - **Tank**: Initial storage tank
4. Click **Save**

## How to Register an Operation

1. Open **Cellar Management > Operations**
2. Click **New Operation**
3. Select operation type:
   - **Racking**: Transfer between tanks
   - **Filtration**: Clarity processing
   - **Bottling**: Bottle filling and corking
   - **Sample**: Lab analysis
4. Select the lot and tank
5. Enter hours and resources used
6. Record any volume changes
7. Click **Save**

## How to Track Consumables

1. Open **Cellar Management > Consumables**
2. View current stock levels
3. Register consumption per operation or batch
4. Check cost per unit

## How to Check Volumes

1. Open **Cellar Management > Inventory**
2. View volumes by:
   - Wine type (Red, White, etc.)
   - Phase (Fermentation, Aging, Bottled)
   - Tank/Barrel

## Onde encontrar na aplicacao

- Intake: **Cellar Management > Intake**
- Lots: **Cellar Management > Lots**
- Operations: **Cellar Management > Operations**
- Inventory: **Cellar Management > Inventory**
